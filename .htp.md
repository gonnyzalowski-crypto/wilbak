## 9Proxy iOS App (ProxyHub Lite)

There **is** an iOS app called **ProxyHub Lite**, but it's in **Developer Preview** — not on the App Store. To install it:

### Requirements
- iPhone/iPad with iOS 15+
- A computer (Windows or Mac)
- An Apple ID (free or paid developer account)

### Installation (easiest method — AltStore)

1. Download the IPA from **https://9proxy.com/download/ios**
2. Install **[AltStore](https://altstore.io/)** on your computer
   - On Windows: install iTunes and iCloud from Apple's website (not Microsoft Store)
3. Connect your iPhone to your computer via cable
4. Open AltServer on computer → **Install AltStore** → select your iPhone
5. On iPhone: **Settings → Privacy & Security → Developer Mode** → enable it
6. Open AltStore on iPhone → import the ProxyHub Lite IPA
7. **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management** → trust the developer profile
8. Open ProxyHub Lite → sign in with your 9Proxy account

### Alternative: Sideloadly
Same process but using [Sideloadly](https://sideloadly.io/) instead of AltStore — drag and drop the IPA, sign with Apple ID, click Install.
