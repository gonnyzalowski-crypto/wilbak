Service Level Agreement

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Our promises for uptime, delivery, and support across all projects and contracts.

1. Uptime Commitment

For systems we build and manage, we aim for at least 99.9% uptime each month. This covers the parts we directly manage as agreed in the project contract. Planned maintenance, events outside our control, and issues caused by third-party providers are not included in uptime calculations. We will let you know about planned maintenance in advance whenever possible.

2. Delivery Standards

  • Project Milestones: Delivery milestones are written into the project plan and tracked against an agreed timeline.
  • Handover: All builds go through testing and documentation before being handed over.
  • Response Times: Initial enquiries are answered within one business day.
  • Change Requests: Requested changes are reviewed and scoped within three business days.

3. Technical Support

Clients work directly with their assigned engineer throughout the project. Urgent issues are addressed within 4 hours during business hours. General questions are answered within one business day. Support after delivery is agreed per project. We do not run a general helpdesk; all support is handled by your assigned engineer.

4. Remedies & Credits

If we fail to meet agreed uptime or delivery targets due to our own fault, we will find the cause and fix it at no extra cost. Service credits or other remedies are agreed on a case-by-case basis in the project contract. Credits do not apply to delays or outages caused by the client, third-party systems, or events outside our control.
