Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

How Wilbak Engineering collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Overview

Wilbak Engineering Group ("Wilbak", "we", "us") cares about your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect from our website and services, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website or sending us an enquiry, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly, such as your name, email address, phone number, and business details through our contact form. We also collect basic technical data from your browser (like IP address, device type, and pages visited) to understand how our site is used. For clients we work with, we may collect extra details needed to plan and deliver the agreed project.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers (like hosting and email services) only to run our website and deliver our services, and they must keep it confidential. We may share information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Wilbak, our clients, or the public.

4. Data Security

We use standard security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. Information submitted through our forms is stored in secure, access-controlled systems. While no system can be perfectly secure, we take data protection just as seriously as every project we deliver.

5. Your Rights & Contact

You can ask to see, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at wilbakengineering.com/contact. We will respond within 30 days. We may update this policy from time to time, and any major changes will be posted on this page with a new date. If you keep using our website after an update, that means you accept the revised policy.
