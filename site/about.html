About the Firm

We build and manage data centres and cloud systems for businesses that want to perform better.

Houston, TX · North America · EMEA

Wilhelm Rybak

Founder & Principal

Wilhelm is the founder and principal of Wilbak Engineering. With over two decades of experience in IT operations, data infrastructure, and systems design, he has delivered projects across North America, Europe, and the Middle East.

His approach comes down to one belief: performance is an engineering problem. Every system he designs is built to fix the root cause — not to cover up symptoms, but to remove the conditions that create them.

His work spans logistics, financial operations, energy infrastructure, and cloud management — always with the same standard: built to last.

"Performance is not a management challenge — it's an engineering problem. Build the right systems and good performance follows naturally."
Wilhelm Rybak — Wilbak Engineering
